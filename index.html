About me

I am a multimedia design student at KEA, Copenhagen school of design and technology, currently on 3rd semester. When I’m not spending time making school projects I love to hang out in my Kolonihave in Skovlunde. It is my sanctuary where I can get my hands dirty when I am doing gardening in my utility garden. It is also where I can open my mind for inspiration and creativity. When I am at home in Copenhagen I love to bike around town on my city-bike and visit all my friends. When I am at home chilling, I try to expand my crocheting-skills while watching the latest TV-series.
